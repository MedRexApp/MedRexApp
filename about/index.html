About PharmaList

Bridging the Gap in Medication Safety

We're on a mission to prevent medication errors and improve patient outcomes through expert pharmacist reviews and intelligent medication management technology.

Our Mission

Every year, millions of patients experience preventable medication errors. These errors occur not because of negligence, but because of a fundamental gap in our healthcare system: no one is looking at the complete picture.

Physicians are focused on treating specific conditions during brief appointments. Retail pharmacists are focused on accurately dispensing medications. But who is reviewing your complete medication list across all your doctors, checking for dangerous interactions, duplicate therapies, and optimization opportunities?

That's where PharmaList comes in.

We connect patients with board-certified clinical pharmacists who have the time, training, and expertise to conduct comprehensive medication reviews. Combined with our intelligent medication management platform, we're making expert pharmaceutical care accessible to everyone.

The Problem We're Solving

Understanding the gaps in medication management

⏱️

Time Constraints

Doctors have 15-20 minutes per appointment. They're focused on your highest priority problems, not comprehensive medication review. Additionally, multiple specialists may not communicate about overlapping therapies.

🏪

Retail Pharmacy Limitations

Retail pharmacists are experts at dispensing meds safely, but they rely on standardized alert systems to process meds quickly. They lack the time and advanced training required to conduct comprehensive medication therapy management effectively.

🔀

Fragmented Care

Patients see multiple doctors who prescribe medications independently. Each assumes the others are monitoring for interactions and duplications. This assumption leads to dangerous gaps.

📋

Lack of Adherence Tracking

Even the best medication regimen fails if not taken correctly. Most patients have no systematic way to track adherence or identify patterns in missed doses.

🔬

Lab-Based Adjustments

Kidney and liver function change over time, requiring dose adjustments. These changes are often missed because no one is actively monitoring the relationship between labs and medications.

💰

Cost Barriers

Clinical pharmacist consultations are often only available through hospitals or expensive concierge services. Most people can't access this level of care.

Our Solution

Comprehensive medication management made accessible

Board-Certified Pharmacist Reviews

Our network of board-certified clinical pharmacists brings hospital-level expertise to your medication list. These pharmacists are medication management specialists who have completed:

  • Doctor of Pharmacy (PharmD) degree
  • Post-graduate residency training (1-2 years) or
  • 4 years clinical practice in direct patient care
  • Board certification examination in specialized areas
  • Continuing education to maintain certification

They work in hospitals and clinics daily, managing complex medication regimens, adjusting therapy, and making clinical recommendations based on lab data and patient outcomes, to optimize patient treatment over time.

Now, through PharmaList, you can access their expertise from home.
