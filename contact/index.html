Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How much does PharmaList cost?

The basic medication management app is free. Comprehensive medication reviews by board-certified pharmacists are available for a fee. Contact us for pricing details.

Is my health data secure?

Yes. We are fully HIPAA compliant with enterprise-grade encryption. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your data to third parties.

Which devices are supported?

Currently available on iPhone, Apple Watch, and Mac. Android and PC versions are coming soon. SMART on FHIR integration with health systems is in development.

How long does a medication review take?

Most comprehensive medication reviews are completed within 48-72 hours. You'll receive a detailed report with recommendations in English or Spanish.

Can I share my medication list with my doctor?

Yes! You can generate printable PDFs of your medication list, adherence reports, and pharmacist review recommendations to share with your healthcare team.

Do I need a prescription to use PharmaList?

No prescription is needed. PharmaList is a medication management tool and consultation service. We don't prescribe or dispense medications.

Are the pharmacists really board-certified?

Yes. Every pharmacist is verified through Credly badge authentication. We confirm active board certification, state licensure, and good standing before allowing reviews.

Can family members help manage my medications?

Yes! You can share your medication list with caregivers and choose whether they can view only or also mark medications as taken. Perfect for elderly parents or children with chronic conditions.

What if I have technical issues?

Contact our support team at support@pharmalist.com or call (555) 123-4568. We offer 24/7 support for urgent issues.
